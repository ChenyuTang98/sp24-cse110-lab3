Agenda 2: Games Preference

Discuss and vote on which games to play at the party.

Popular Games:

We have several game options to consider for the party, catering to different preferences and group sizes:

  • Board games: Ideal for small groups, we can consider classics like 'Monopoly', 'Catan', or 'Risk' for strategic fun, or 'Pictionary' and 'Charades' for something more dynamic.
  • Card games: 'Poker', 'Blackjack', or more casual games like 'Uno' can provide hours of entertainment. We can also include traditional card games from various cultures.
  • Video games: Set up gaming stations with popular titles like 'Mario Kart', 'FIFA', or 'Super Smash Bros.' for competitive play, or 'Just Dance' for a more active and inclusive option.

Agenda 3: Lotteries Gifts Preference

Types of gifts for the lottery draw.

We aim to make the lottery draw exciting and rewarding for everyone. Here are some gift options considering:

  • Electronic Gadgets: Items like smart watches, headphones, or Bluetooth speakers are always popular and can appeal to a tech-savvy crowd.
  • Gift Cards: These can range from online shopping vouchers to dining or spa experiences, offering winners a variety of choices.
  • Personalized Items: Customized gifts such as mugs, t-shirts, or photo frames that can be personalized for the winners on the spot.
  • Books and Stationery: For the book lovers and those who appreciate writing, a selection of bestsellers and quality stationery can be included.
